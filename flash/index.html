Meme Coin Holding Period & Wallet Requirements

To ensure account security:

  • You must hold meme coins (such as PEPE, DOGE, SHIBA) for at least 24 hours before selling them
  • USDT FLASH should be deposited in a WEB3 wallet (like MetaMask, Trust Wallet)
  • If you deposit USDT FLASH directly into a trading platform, it will disappear after 1 hour

Critical Warning: DO NOT

Never attempt to:

  • Sell USDT FLASH directly on a trading platform
  • Transfer USDT FLASH to someone else using their ID or username
